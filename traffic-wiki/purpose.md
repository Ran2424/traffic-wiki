# Traffic Wiki Purpose

Build a lightweight, evidence-linked, English-first candidate knowledge graph for the history of traffic engineering.

The extraction layer records people, organizations, events, works, concepts, methods, models, algorithms, metrics, tools, technologies, policies, standards, projects, places, and named periods. It focuses on roads, streets, traffic flow, control, planning, safety, public transport, urban mobility, and directly relevant interfaces with other transport modes.

This version extracts knowledge only. It does not write a comprehensive history, choose between disputed interpretations, or treat model output as verified truth.
